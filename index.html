Spiderman: Across the Spiderverse


Character Picture

Character Information

Miles Morales


Miles Morales is one of the main characters of the film when the woman he likes, Gwen Stacy, steals him to go to spider society and tries to do everything in his power to save his dad.


Gwen Stacy


Gwen Stacy is one of Mile's closest girl friends. She didn't talk to him for like a while until Spiderman 2099 and his people tried to plan something bad for Miles Morales, leading her to talk to Miles again. Right after the incident where Miles run and tried to escape, she felt bad and did everything just to see and help him save his dad.


Spiderman 2099


Spiderman 2099 is the antagonist of the film. He is the leader of the spider-society, and his real name is Miguel O'Hara. He has variants but this version is different than the others, giving him Spiderman-like powers.


Jefferson Davis


Jefferson Davis is the father of Miles Morales, aka Spiderman. He is a police, and hes the type of father that casually worries about his son.


Peter Parker with baby


Peter Parker with baby is a spiderman variant that came from another world. In the first movie, he was single and got divorced by his wife until he patched up things with her and had a baby. What is hilarious about this new version of this character is that whenever he has a mission, he brings his baby.
